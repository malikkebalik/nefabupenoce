How Did Dom Famularo Die? American Renowned Drummer Dies At 70

The world of music was left in mourning on Wednesday, September, 27th, 2023, when news broke of the passing of Dom Famularo, the legendary American drummer who touched countless lives with his talent and passion for percussion. Famularo’s death at the age of 70 was a significant loss to the music industry, as he had not only made a name for himself as a remarkable drummer but also as a mentor and educator. 

Keep reading the article to know more about Dom Famularo, including who he was, how he died, what was the cause of his death, tributes for him, and much more!

Who Was Dom Famularo?

Dominic Famularo, affectionately known as “Dom,” was born on June 13, 1953, in Long Island, New York. From a young age, he displayed a deep passion for drumming, and his journey towards becoming a renowned drummer began early. Over the years, Famularo honed his skills, exploring various styles and techniques that would later define his distinctive drumming style.

Famularo’s career took flight in the 1970s, as he began performing with numerous bands and artists, showcasing his versatility and technical skills. His talent and infectious enthusiasm for drumming quickly made him a sought-after session and live drummer. Throughout his career, he collaborated with notable musicians such as Billy Cobham, Joe Franco, and Johnny Cash, leaving his mark on a wide range of musical genres, from jazz to rock to country.

While Dom Famularo’s drumming talent was undeniable, it was his passion for teaching and mentoring that truly set him apart. Famularo firmly believed in the power of education and dedicated himself to sharing his knowledge and passion for drumming with aspiring musicians worldwide.

One of Famularo’s most significant contributions to the world of drumming was his work as an educator. He conducted countless drum clinics, workshops, and masterclasses, sharing his insights and techniques with eager learners of all ages. His ability to connect with students and inspire them to reach their full potential earned him the reputation of a drumming guru.

How Did Dom Famularo Die? What Happened to Him?

Dom Famularo, an iconic American drummer who left an indelible mark on the industry sadly passed away on Wednesday, September 27, 2023, at the age of 70.

Dom may have left this world, but his spirit lives on in the hearts and rhythms of those who were fortunate enough to be touched by his music and his life. Throughout his battle with stage 3 pancreatic cancer, he continued to inspire those around him, proving that his strength and resilience extended beyond the drum kit.

Mike Portnoy was the one who first shared the news of Dom Famularo’s passing, and also penned a heartfelt tribute for him, saying

“I was heartbroken to learn of the death of the great Dom Famularo, a drummer who was also a friend and a real being. Dom was undoubtedly one of them when “the good guys” are spoken of. A lovely soul and has such a good vibe. No one on earth was a better motivator and someone who could help anyone.”

Dom Famularo Battle With Pancreatic Cancer

In May 2022, the music world received the devastating news that Dom Famularo had been diagnosed with stage 3 pancreatic cancer. The announcement sent shockwaves throughout the drumming community, with countless fans and colleagues expressing their support and best wishes for the beloved drummer.

But there was an element of hope in September. After receiving chemotherapy for six years, Famularo got a CT scan that revealed he was cancer-free. His family experienced great delight and relief at this news as he was on the road to recovery and regaining his life.

Sadly on September 27th, despite his brave efforts, Famularo lost his battle with the illness. This moving journey, from the first cancer diagnosis to the joyful declaration of being cancer-free and, ultimately, to his departure, serves as an unsettling reminder of life’s unpredictability.

Tributes for Dom Famularo

Since Dom Famularo’s death came into existence, many of his friends, family, and loved ones took to various social media platforms to express their disbelief about his death, and to pay tribute to him.

Todd Sucherman posted on Facebook, saying “RIP, Dom Famularo, the one and only. Awful news. Ambassador for drumming around the world, master of ceremonies at nearly all drum festivals, drummer, instructor, and lecturer, as well as husband and parent. One of the most motivating and upbeat people I have ever encountered.”

In a post, Claus Hessler mentioned, “For me, it was a terrible day since it was another strike brought on by 2023. One of the most prominent drum professors of our time has left the drumming community. One of my closest pals has passed away. Although it is difficult to accept that his voice will never be heard again, I am confident that his message and vision for drumming and education will continue to resonate around the globe.”

Vinnie Colaiuta stated, “We lost the wonderful global drumming ambassador Dom Famularo today. He was a wealth of information, a source of ongoing inspiration for all of us, and always upbeat, encouraging everyone to be upbeat.  He was a friend and a colleague to innumerable people, including me, and his absence will be deeply felt.”

The passing of Dom Famularo at the age of 70 marked the end of an era in the world of drumming. His talent, dedication, and passion for percussion left an indelible mark on the music industry. As a mentor and educator, he empowered countless drummers to find their rhythm and reach for the stars. His legacy extends far beyond his musical achievements, serving as a source of inspiration for all who had the privilege of knowing him.
