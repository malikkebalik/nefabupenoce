Biography

Patrick Carney was born in Akron, OH on April 15, 1980. Drummer and member of the blues rock duo known as The Black Keys. He also formed the side rock project Drummer in 2009 and has been the founder of Audio Eagle Records. He produced Michelle Branch‘s Hopeless Romantic album, which is around the time the two originally started dating. He played tag football with his bandmate Dan Auerbach when he was young and they later became close friends.
