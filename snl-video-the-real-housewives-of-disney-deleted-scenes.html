SNL VIDEO The Real Housewives of Disney deleted scenes

Saturday Night Live hit a comedy grand slam with their Disneyfied take on Bravo’s Real Housewives reality show franchise as Cinderella, Snow White, Jasmine, Belle, Rapunzel and Prince Charming doled out some of that fabled diva drama we’ve all grown to love in “The Real Housewives of Disney!”

(CLICK HERE to watch the original clip!)

The comedy short was such a hit that NBC has now released a short clip of outtakes and deleted scenes from it that includes Kristen Wiig as Cinderella doing her best Sheree Whitfield impersonation by yanking Rapunzel’s (played by Lindsay Lohan) hair and declaring, “Oh I’m gonna rip this fake ass weave out of your head!”

This needs to be its own weekly comedy series!
