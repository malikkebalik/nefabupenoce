Val Mooty's Parents Are Already Divorced 

Val Mooty's father, Jerry Mooty, and Catherine Mooty are no longer in a husband-wife relationship. The duo has not disclosed the marriage date, but they lived a blissful marital life for more than a decade.

Catherine and Jerry shared custody of their children equally. Even though the two children live with Catherine, both look after them.

Musician Val Mooty's Step-Dad And Step-Mom
