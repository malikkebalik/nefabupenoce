Zhao Li Ying is a Chinese actress and singer known for portraying various important roles in popular TV shows and films like Lu Zhen, Boss & Me, Duckweed, and The Monkey King 3. She has done endorsement work for numerous brands like Dior, Longines, Dove, and Jasmine Tea. Zhao has lent her voice to songs like Mood, Destruction in Chaos, and I Have Never Existed.

    Zhao Li Ying Facts

  • She started her show business career in 2006 by winning the Yahoo Search Star Game competition. After her win in the talent competition, she signed a representation contract with Huayi Brothers as a fresh talent. She has been pursued by numerous high profile agencies.
  • In 2016, she was appointed as the tourism ambassador of the northern Chinese province, Hebei.
  • While studying in high school, she performed in a couple of plays and subsequently got interested in acting. But, her parents weren’t in favor of dropping studies to start her acting career. Therefore, she had to wait for the completion of her studies.
  • In 2010, she got her first big breakthrough with her role in the popular TV series, The Dream of Red Mansions, which was based on a novel with a similar title.
  • In May 2017, her wax figure was unveiled at the Madame Tussauds museum in Beijing.
  • Zhao was included by Forbes in their “30 Under 30 Asia 2017” list, which featured thirty individuals under the age of 30, who had achieved success in their respective fields.
