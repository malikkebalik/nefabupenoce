Ben Savage Bio (Wiki)

Who is Ben Savage?

Ben Savage is a popular American actor. He is popular for his acting skills in Boy Meets World (1993–2000) and Girl Meets World (2014–2017).

Recently in 2022, he played the role of David Singer in Love, Lights, Hanukkah! (TV Movie) .

Ben Savage- Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

His birthdate is 13 Sep 1980 and he was born in Chicago, Illinois, the US as Bennett Joseph Savage. The name of his mother is Joanne Savage and his father is Lewis Savage.

Ben’s ancestry is a mix of Polish-Ukrainian-German-Latvian and has a brother Fred Savage who is a musician. His sister Kala Savage is an actress by profession.

Regarding his academics, Ben Savage first passed out from Brentwood School and then he enrolled at Stanford University. From the latter, he obtained a degree in Political Science in 2004.

Ben Savage is a popular face of the entertainment world. Till now, he has shown his acting skills in several movies as well as TV shows.
