Nikkita Lyons Facts

  • She was brought up in Hollywood, California.
  • Nikkita took up Taekwondo when she was just 4 years old and by the age of 8, she had already earned her black belt.
  • She made her wrestling debut on October 11, 2018, under the ring name “Faith The Lioness” at the Women of Wrestling promotion.
  • Lyons got her first break in the WWE while attending their summer tryouts in 2021, held in Las Vegas where she managed to successfully get a contract. She went on to make her debut on the December 31, 2021, episode of 205 Live, where she lost to Amari Miller.
  • On February 22, 2022, Nikkita made her debut under the NXT brand winning against Kayla Inlay.
  • Nikkita has a pet dog named Rockette. She also had another dog named RockDud who sadly passed away.
  • She bought herself a car from the company Jaguar in July 2023.
  • Under the name Faithy J, she has released various tracks including Baby U and No Time (feat. La Jones).
