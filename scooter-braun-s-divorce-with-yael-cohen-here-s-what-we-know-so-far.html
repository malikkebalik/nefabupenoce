Scooter Brauns Divorce with Yael Cohen Heres What We Know So Far

Scott Samuel Braun, better known as Scooter Braun, is a record executive and media proprietor. He manages various A-listers in the music industry like Ariana Grande, Demi Lovato, J Balvin, Justin Bieber, and The Kid LAROI, among others. 

Braun is also a successful entrepreneur with various ventures like Schoolboy Records, TQ Ventures, Ithaca Ventures, RBMG Records, Mythos Studios, and Esports team named 100 Thieves. 

Since 2019, Braun has made the news because of his high-profile feud with Grammy-winning artist Taylor Swift. Nowadays, his personal life seems to have taken over his professional one. 

Braun is separating from his wife of seven years, Yael Cohen. To the surprise of many, he deleted his social media on July 26, 2021, following the confirmation of divorce. 

Scott Braun and Yael Cohen’s Divorce

Braun and Cohen started dating in 2013 after the media mogul attended Cohen's Ted Talk about her organization. Within a year of dating, the pair got engaged in January 2014. 

They got married six months later, on July 6, 2014, at Cohen's parents' home in British Columbia. The duo welcomed their first son in February 2015 and a second son in November 2016. The pair gave birth to their third child and first daughter on December 1, 2018. 

On July 11, 2021, TMZ reported the pair were trying their best to save their marriage. Then, there was no mention of divorce, but just the fact that they had a fallout and were working things out. 

They were still posting pictures of one another on their Instagram, and Braun even celebrated Cohen on their seventh wedding anniversary. In the now-deleted post, he thanked Cohen for helping him become the best version of himself. 

Cohen replied in the comments, "A Team 4lyfe," hinting that the pair were still on good terms and perhaps, together. Unfortunately, that is not the case anymore. 

On July 21, 2021, TMZ exclusively noted that the celebrity manager had filed for divorce with the help of his lawyer Laura Wasser.

According to the reports, Braun has asked for joint custody for three kids and has agreed to pay Cohen the approved spousal support. 

Similarly, there is a prenup drawn by Wasser for dividing their assets therefore, there are high chances that two will walk away with their own assets. However, the date of separation has not been announced. 

Is Scooter Braun Dating Erika Jayne?

Erika Jayne is an actress, singer, and television personality best known for The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills (RHOBH). She married an attorney and a founding partner of Girardi & Keese, Thomas Girardi, in 2000. 

However, after over two decades of marriage, the pair called it quits in November 2020. Recently, Jayne was dubbed as Braun's girlfriend, but the RHOBH star debunked all the rumors on the reality show's June 30, 2021 episode. 

Jayne addressed the rumors with RHOBH co-stars Garcelle Beauvais and Lisa Rinna. Beauvais asked her if she visited an erotic shop with the celebrity manager, to which Jayne brushed it off and called the allegations "silly." 

She confirmed she had not met Braun for the last four or five years. On July 1, Page Six also reported that sources close to music mogul had told them that the rumor was false. 

Braun and Jayne met each other when Jayne attended a political event with her then-husband, Girardi. However, since then, they have not come across each other.
