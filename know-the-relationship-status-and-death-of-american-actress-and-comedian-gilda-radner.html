Know the Relationship Status and Death of American Actress and Comedian, Gilda Radner

If you have ever watched American movies and comedies of the late 1980s then, Gilda Radner must not be a new name to you. The multi-talented Emmy Award Winner, Gilda Radner, is widely popular as the original cast member of the NBC sketch comedy show Saturday Night Live (SNL).

Gilda was born as Gilda Susan Radner on June 28, 1946, in Detroit, Michigan. She is one of the two children born to Herman Radner and his wife, Henrietta Dworkin. She has a brother named Michael. Her father was a well-known Business executive, and her mother was a secretary. She graduated from University Liggett School. 

Death of Gilda Radner

Yes, You read that right, Gilda is no more with us at present. Gilda left this beautiful world on May 20, 1989, at the age of forty-two. Gilda passed away battling with Ovarian Cancer.

She was diagnosed with cancer in October of 1986. Soon after the incident, she started undergoing chemotherapy. However, it could not be controlled and went to coma at the beginning of May. She regained consciousness and took her last breathe after three days on May 20. 

Relationship History of Gilda Radner

Along with her successful life career, Gilda was famous for her relationship. Gilda's relationship started in the late 1970s. She first tied her knot with her first husband, G.E Smith, in 1980. The two had been dating for some time before tying their knots.

However, this relationship short-lived, and the two separated in 1982. They had their official divorce the same year. The two did not have any children from this relationship.

Soon after this first heartbreak, Gilda met Gene Wilder. The two had love at first sight and started living together. They formally tied their knot on September 18, 1984, in Saint-Tropez. The two remained together until the death of Radner in 1989. 

Net Worth and Total Earnings of Gilda 

Gilda Radner had an estimated net worth of close to $4 Million at the time of her death. She had accumulated most of her net worth through her successful career of working as a actress and a comedian. Mo'Nique is another American comedian and a actress with a net worth of $20 Million.

Awards and Recognition

Radner received most of her prestigious awards after her death. In 1977, Gilda won an Emmy Award for Outstanding Continuing or Single Performance by a Supporting Actress in Variety or Music" for her performance on Saturday Night Live.

Just After her death on 1990, Gilda received a Grammy Award for "Best Spoken Word Or Non-Musical Recording." On June 27, 2003, Radner received her star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. In 1992, she was inducted into the Michigan Woman's Hall of Fame.
