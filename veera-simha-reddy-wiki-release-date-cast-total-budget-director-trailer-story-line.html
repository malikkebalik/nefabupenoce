Veera Simha Reddy Wiki, Release Date, Cast, Total Budget, Director, Trailer, Story Line

Veera Simha Reddy Wiki:- Veera Simha Reddy (Release date: 12 January 2023) is a new upcoming Indian Telugu-language action masala movie directed by Gopichand Malineni and produced by Mythri Movie Makers. This movie stars Nandamuri Balakrishna in the titular role alongside Shruti Hassan, Varalaxmi Sarathkumar, Honey Rose, and Duniya Vijay.

Here we discuss Veera Simha Reddy Wiki, Release Date, Cast, Total Budget, Director, Trailer, Story Line & More.

Release Date

Veera Simha Reddy is released on 12 January 2023 coinciding with Sankranti. It is available in theaters in Telugu-language.
