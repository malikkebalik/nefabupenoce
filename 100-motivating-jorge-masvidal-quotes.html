He has also played professionally for Bellator, Strikeforce, Shark Fights, and World Victory Road. Jorge Masvidal also set the record for the fastest knockout in UFC history at 5 seconds.

Also, on March 4, 2019, he is #11 in the official UFC welterweight rankings. Later, on July 14, 2020, he became #4 in the UFC welterweight rankings.
