Watch Rolling Loud Bay Area 2017 Livestream

Mountain View, CA - 

Revolt TV is livestreaming Rolling Loud Bay Area 2017 for all those who can’t make it to the festival. The first day of the event is headlined by Travis Scott, Lil Uzi Vert, Playboi Carti and 21 Savage.

Saturday’s (October 21) show is jam-packed with notable performers, including Lil B, G Herbo, XXXTENTACION, A Boogie Wit Da Hoodie, Ugly God, Nef The Pharoah, Chaz French, Maxo Kream and Seshollowaterboyz (Bones, Xavier Wulf, Chris Travis and Eddy Baker). Emerging artists like SOBxRBE, Trippie Redd and Smokepurpp are also on the bill.

AD LOADING...

The Bay Area iteration of Rolling Loud is the first part of a global expansion by the Miami-based festival. Organizers are running another event in California in December before moving to shows in the United Kingdom, China and Japan in 2018.
