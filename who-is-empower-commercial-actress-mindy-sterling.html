New Empower TV Ad, ‘Awkward Question’: Explained

The “What’s Next” campaign, featuring actress Mindy, unfolds a narrative centered around financial empowerment and the transformative role of the Empower platform in alleviating financial concerns.

The ad begins with Mindy enjoying a serene poolside moment with her daughter until an awkward financial query disrupts the tranquility.

This prompts a flashback to a year prior, depicting Mindy attempting to organize her finances, frustrated by her children’s lack of financial understanding.

Returning to the present, Mindy, lounging by the pool with her daughter, recalls that Empower comprehensively addresses their financial inquiries, ensuring a worry-free vacation.

The narrative then shifts to another individual in an office setting expressing frustration about her children’s uncertain financial preferences, underscoring the relatable challenges faced by parents.

The ad culminates with Mindy confidently asserting that the company resolves all their financial queries, thereby eliminating concerns.

The male voice-over artist delivers the campaign’s tagline, “Empower, what’s next,” encapsulating the idea that Empower is the catalyst for moving forward with financial planning and management.

The campaign strategically positions itself as a reliable solution for individuals seeking financial clarity and empowerment in their decision-making processes.

Mindy Sterling Biography

Mindy Lee Sterling, born on July 11, 1953, in Paterson, New Jersey, began her artistic career in the early 1970s.

Sterling’s early career laid the framework for her eventual rise to comic recognition, from a recurring role on the syndicated children’s series Dusty’s Treehouse to membership in the prestigious L.A.-based comedy group, The Groundlings.

Outstanding Contributions in Film and Television

Sterling has a lengthy filmography, with her role as Frau Farbissina in Austin Powers: International Man of Mystery catapulting her to notoriety. Recurring characters on popular sitcoms like iCarly, A.N.T. Farm, and The Goldbergs showcase her ability to seamlessly transition between roles.

The Breast Cancer Journey: Overcoming Adversity

When Mindy was diagnosed with breast cancer in 1998, she faced enormous personal adversity. Undaunted, she persevered through treatment, eventually becoming a symbol of courage and resilience.

Professional Legacies That Are Still In Use

Empower: A Legacy of Financial Empowerment

Empower, a financial holding company situated in Greenwood Village, Colorado, has a rich history dating back to 1891 as part of Great-West Lifeco.

Empower, managed by President and CEO Edmund F. Murphy III, has accomplished significant milestones such as strategic acquisitions and a 21-year naming rights contract for the Denver Broncos stadium.

Expansion and Commitment to Strategic Expansion

Because of a record of strategic expansion and commitment to financial empowerment, It has acquired significant market competitors, managing over $1.0 trillion in assets for millions of individuals and organization retirement plan participants.
