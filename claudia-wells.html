She was replaced by Elisabeth Shue in the two sequels. She appeared in Fast Times, a television series based on the 1982 film Fast Times at Ridgemont High. She played Linda Barrett, a role filled in the film by actress Phoebe Cates. She appeared in television shows while attending high school in San Francisco.
