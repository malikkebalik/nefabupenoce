Is Saint X Based On True Story?

Is Saint X Based On True Story? No, Saint X is not based on a true story.

The fictional psychological drama series is based on a novel of the same name by Alexis Schaitkin. Leila Gerstein is the creator of the show. It started with its first episode, " A Lovely Nowhere," which premiered on Hulu in April 2023.

The soap opera narrates a tale about a girl whose life takes a completely different turn after losing her older sister when she was a child. After growing up into an independent woman, she tries to solve the mystery by herself.

The television sitcom has featured actors like Alycia Debnam-Carey, Jayden Elijah, Betsy Brandt, and many more. There are six writers for the whole chapter of the series, including Leila herself and Matthew Cruz.

What Is Saint X About?

Saint X plot summary circulates around a girl who tries to solve the mystery of her older sister murder. Emily was just seven when she lost her sister.

The narration begins with the Thomas family's trip to a luxurious vacation on a fictional Caribbean Island. Their daughters Alison and Claire join them. They unpack their belongings and start exploring the surrounding beauty.

The following day, a college student, Alison, plays volleyball with male guests. Later that night, she meets Tyler in private. She doesn't return till late at night, and her parents are worried for her.

The following morning, they find out someone has murdered their kid. The local detective suspects Edwin and Gogo( Clive) of the crime. Yet, the case doesn't solve there.

It has been around fifteen years since the incident took place. It's still a mystery about what happened that day. Claire (mostly called by her nickname Emily) has grown up now. She works as an editor for environmental documentary films.
