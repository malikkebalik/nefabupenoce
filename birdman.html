Birdman is an entrepreneur, rapper, and record executive from the United States. He is the co-founder and public face of Cash Money Records, which his older brother, Ronald “Slim” Williams, started in 1992. He has four studio albums to his credit and was also a member of the hip-hop group Big Tymers alongside Mannie Fresh.

Apart from performing with the Big Tymers and as a solo artist, he is also recognized for collaborating with Lil Wayne on a number of tracks and an album. When Lil Wayne was a kid, Birdman noticed him and took him under his wing. He also established the Rich Gang hip-hop supergroup, which helped musicians like Young Thug and Rich Homie Quan advance their careers.
